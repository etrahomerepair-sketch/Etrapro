FLOORING TYPES WE INSTALL
◈
Vinyl Plank Flooring
Durable, waterproof, and available in a wide range of wood-look finishes. Vinyl plank is one of the most popular choices for Halifax homeowners -- it handles moisture well and stands up to heavy foot traffic, making it ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and entryways.
◈
Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)
A step up in thickness, texture, and realism. LVP offers a premium look and feel with enhanced durability, and it is a smart choice for high-traffic areas in both homes and commercial spaces. Click-lock or glue-down -- we do both.
◈
Hardwood Flooring
Timeless beauty and long-term value. We install solid hardwood and engineered hardwood with precision fitting, proper expansion gaps, and clean transitions. A natural choice for living rooms, dining areas, and bedrooms.
◈
Tile Flooring
Porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone tile installation. Perfect for bathrooms, kitchens, mudrooms, and commercial entrances. We ensure level surfaces, consistent grout lines, and sealed finishes that last.
◈
Laminate Flooring
An affordable alternative that still delivers a clean, modern look. Laminate installs quickly and works well for bedrooms, offices, and rental units where budget and durability need to balance.
Preparation Matters

SUBFLOOR
REPAIR &
PREP.

A great floor starts with what is underneath it. Many flooring problems -- squeaks, uneven surfaces, warping, and premature wear -- come down to a subfloor that was not properly prepared before installation. We address these issues before your new flooring goes down, not after.

Whether it is an older home in Halifax's South End that needs leveling compound, or a new construction project in Fall River that needs moisture testing, our team handles the preparation work that keeps your floors performing for years to come.

WHAT WE HANDLE
Subfloor leveling and smoothing
Damaged subfloor replacement
Moisture testing and vapor barriers
Old flooring and adhesive removal
Plywood and OSB subfloor repair
Transition strips and trim installation
Underlayment selection and installation
Expansion gap planning for seasonal movement
No Surprises

If we find subfloor issues during your project, we discuss the problem and the solution with you before any extra work begins. Our quotes are honest and transparent -- that is how we do business across every project in HRM.
