Nosing Profiles

TWO PROFILES.
EVERY STAIR
TYPE COVERED.

Our stair nosing profiles are engineered for precision installation and a clean, professional finish. Choose the right model based on your flooring application and stair configuration. Both profiles are available in professional and consumer grades.

Model 01
FEMALE CLICK
Vinyl Floor Application

Designed for standard vinyl floor applications. The female click mechanism locks directly into the nosing profile — no cutting or scoring of the vinyl material is required. Ideal for straightforward installs with a clean, seamless transition.

Profile 2" nosing depth
Click Type Female Click — locks into nosing
Application Vinyl floor applications
Cutting No cutting or scoring required
Grades Professional & Consumer
Inquire About This Model  →
Model 02
MALE CLICK / DECO MODEL
Deco Model Application

Designed for stairs with an overhang that needs to be filled before installation. The male click mechanism provides a secure connection with a slightly larger 2.25" profile depth, delivering a flush, seamless result on any stair configuration.

Profile 2.25" nosing depth
Click Type Male Click — extends into flooring
Application Deco model / overhang stairs
Prep Overhang must be filled before install
