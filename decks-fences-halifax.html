Deck & Fence Services

EXTEND YOUR
LIVING SPACE.

"A well-built deck or fence doesn't just add square footage to your home — it changes how you live in it."

ETRA Pro Services builds custom decks and installs fences across Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, and the entire Halifax Regional Municipality. Whether you need a new backyard deck for entertaining, a privacy fence to define your property line, or repairs to an aging structure, we bring the same attention to detail and honest pricing to every outdoor project.

We work with pressure-treated lumber, composite decking, and cedar — matching the right material to your goals, your home's style, and your budget. Every project starts with a free on-site quote and ends with a structure built to last through years of East Coast weather.

Deck Construction

CUSTOM
DECK BUILDS

From ground-level platforms to multi-level entertainment decks, we design and build outdoor spaces that fit your property and how you actually use it. Our team handles everything from footings and framing to the finished surface, so you get a single point of contact from start to finish.

Halifax homeowners know that outdoor structures need to stand up to fog, salt air, freeze-thaw cycles, and heavy snow loads. Every deck we build in Dartmouth, Bedford, and across HRM is engineered for the local climate — not just built to code, but built to perform.

♦
Pressure-Treated Lumber
The most popular choice for Halifax decks. Affordable, durable, and treated to resist rot, insects, and moisture. Ideal for homeowners who want a solid deck at a practical price point.
♦
Composite Decking
Low-maintenance boards that won't splinter, warp, or fade. No staining, no sealing — just enjoy it. Perfect for busy families and properties in Dartmouth and Bedford where you want the look of wood without the upkeep.
♦
Cedar Decks
Naturally rot-resistant with a beautiful warm tone. Cedar is a premium choice that ages gracefully and offers a distinctive look that sets your home apart.
♦
Complete Build Service
Footings, framing, decking, stairs, railings, and finishing details. We handle permits, layout, and the full build — you just tell us what you want.
♦
Privacy Fences
Solid panel construction that blocks sightlines and creates a private backyard retreat. The most requested fence style across Halifax and HRM for good reason — it works.
♦
Picket Fences
Classic styling for front yards and gardens. We build traditional and modern picket designs that add curb appeal and define your property without closing it off.
♦
Board-on-Board
Overlapping boards that look great from both sides — no "bad" side for your neighbour. A popular upgrade across Bedford and Dartmouth neighbourhoods for its clean, finished appearance.
♦
Custom Designs
Horizontal slat, lattice-top, mixed-material, and custom gate designs. If you have a vision, we can build it. We work with you to match your home's architecture and your property's layout.

Repairs & Railings

DECK REPAIRS
& RAILINGS

Not every project needs a full rebuild. If your deck has soft spots, loose boards, or damaged railings, we can repair and reinforce the existing structure — saving you money while extending its life for years to come. Our team assesses the full condition of your deck before recommending a repair or replacement.

We also install and replace railings on both new and existing decks across Halifax and HRM. Whether you want wood, aluminum, or glass panel railings, we make sure everything is secure, level, and code-compliant.

Board replacement and resurfacing
Structural joist and beam repair
Post and footing reinforcement
Stair rebuilds and tread replacement
Wood, aluminum, and glass railing installs
Fence post replacement and panel repairs
Full teardown and disposal of old structures

♦
When to Repair vs. Replace
If the footings and framing are sound, a deck resurface or targeted repair is usually the smarter move. We give you an honest assessment — we won't recommend a full rebuild when a repair will do the job.
♦
Railing Upgrades
Swapping old, weathered railings for new aluminum or glass panels is one of the fastest ways to modernize a deck. It changes the entire look — and often improves the view from inside your home too.

Serving all of HRM: We handle deck and fence repairs in Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, Sackville, Fall River, Cole Harbour, Eastern Passage, Timberlea, Hammonds Plains, and surrounding communities.
