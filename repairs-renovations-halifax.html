What We Repair & Renovate

OUR REPAIR &
RENOVATION SERVICES

From a single fix to a complete room transformation, here's what we handle for homeowners across Halifax and HRM.

☑
Punch-List Repairs

All those little things that have been piling up: squeaky doors, cracked trim, loose railings, scuffed walls, sticky drawers. We knock out your entire list efficiently so you can stop thinking about it. Popular across Halifax and Dartmouth for move-in and move-out touch-ups.

■
Single-Room Renovations

Want to transform a spare bedroom, refresh a dated living room, or convert a basement space? We handle full single-room renovations from demo to finishing touches. New flooring, fresh drywall, updated trim, paint-ready walls. One room at a time, done right.

◇
Bathroom Remodels

From vanity swaps and tile updates to complete gut-and-rebuild bathroom renovations. We handle flooring, walls, fixtures, and finishing. Whether it's a half-bath refresh in Bedford or a full ensuite remodel in Halifax, we deliver clean, modern results.

◆
Kitchen Updates

Not every kitchen needs a $60K renovation. We specialize in smart, targeted kitchen updates: new backsplash, cabinet refacing, countertop replacement, updated hardware, and flooring transitions. Real improvements that change the feel of the room without blowing your budget.

✎
General Handyman Work

Shelving, door installs, weatherstripping, fixture swaps, minor framing, closet organizers, and more. If you've got a project that needs a skilled set of hands, we're the call to make. Serving all of HRM with reliable, same-week scheduling whenever possible.

●
Water Damage Repair

Water damage doesn't wait, and neither should your repair. We handle drywall replacement, subfloor repair, mould remediation prep, trim restoration, and moisture-damaged flooring throughout Halifax and Dartmouth. We get to the root of the issue and fix it properly.

▵
Trim & Finishing Work

Baseboards, crown moulding, window casings, door trim, wainscoting, and custom millwork finishing. The trim is what makes a room look finished and professional. Whether you're completing a renovation or upgrading the details in an older Halifax home, our trim work is precise and paint-ready. We take pride in tight mitre cuts, seamless joints, and clean lines that bring a space together.
