Exterior Siding

YOUR HOME'S
FIRST LINE
OF DEFENCE.

Living in Halifax means your home's exterior takes a beating. Between coastal winds off the harbour, heavy winter snowfall, and spring rain, your siding has to do more than look good — it has to perform. ETRA Pro Services provides professional siding installation and repair across Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, and the wider HRM.

We work with vinyl, composite, and wood siding to suit every budget and aesthetic. Whether you're re-siding an older home, building new, or patching storm damage, our team delivers clean installs with proper flashing, moisture barriers, and tight finishing that keeps the weather where it belongs — outside.

Every project starts with a free on-site assessment. We walk the exterior with you, identify problem areas, talk through material options, and provide an honest quote with no hidden costs.

What We Install

SIDING
OPTIONS

We install and repair a range of exterior siding materials. Each option has its strengths — we help you choose what makes sense for your home and your budget.

◆
Vinyl Siding

The most popular choice for homes across Halifax and Dartmouth. Vinyl siding is low-maintenance, weather-resistant, and available in a wide range of colours and profiles. It handles Nova Scotia's freeze-thaw cycles well and won't rot, peel, or need repainting. An excellent balance of durability and value.

◆
Composite Siding

Engineered for performance, composite siding offers the natural appearance of wood with significantly better resistance to moisture and insects. It's a strong choice for homeowners in Bedford, Sackville, and coastal areas of HRM where humidity and salt air accelerate wear on traditional materials.

◆
Wood Siding

Classic character that nothing else replicates. Cedar and spruce siding bring warmth and authenticity to heritage homes and custom builds. We handle proper installation with appropriate sealing and ventilation to maximise its lifespan in Nova Scotia's demanding coastal climate.

Built for This Climate

NOVA SCOTIA
WEATHER.
HANDLED.

Halifax homes face conditions that many parts of Canada simply don't deal with — the combination of ocean moisture, heavy freeze-thaw cycling, and strong coastal winds creates a uniquely demanding environment for any exterior cladding. Siding that works in Ontario won't necessarily hold up here without proper installation technique.

That's where local experience matters. Our crew understands the specific challenges of siding homes in HRM, from the salt air corrosion common in Dartmouth and Eastern Passage to the wind exposure on hilltop properties in Bedford and Hammonds Plains. We don't just hang panels — we build a weather-tight envelope around your home.
