Whether you're building from scratch, finishing a renovation, or patching up damage from years of everyday life, drywall is one of those things that has to be done right. It's the surface you see every single day -- and when it's done well, you don't even think about it. That's the goal.

ETRA Pro Services provides full drywall installation and repair for homeowners and businesses across Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, Sackville, Cole Harbour, and the greater HRM region. From hanging new sheets in a basement renovation to patching a hole in the hallway, we bring the same level of care and precision to every project.

NEW DRYWALL INSTALLATION

Starting a new build or renovating an existing space? We handle complete drywall installation from framing to finish. That includes measuring, cutting, hanging, taping, mudding, and sanding -- everything it takes to give you smooth, paint-ready walls and ceilings.

We work with general contractors on new construction projects and directly with homeowners on renovations. Whether it's a single room addition, a full basement finish, or a multi-unit commercial buildout, we've got the experience and the crew to get it done on schedule.

DRYWALL REPAIR & PATCHING

Holes, cracks, water damage, dents from furniture -- drywall takes a beating over time, and it shows. We handle repairs of all sizes, from small nail holes and doorknob dents to large sections damaged by leaks or plumbing work.

Our repair process isn't just about filling holes. We match the existing surface so the repair blends in completely. When we're done, you shouldn't be able to tell where the damage was.

TAPING, MUDDING & FINISHING

The finish is where drywall work lives or dies. Bad taping and mudding shows through every coat of paint, and once it's painted, it's too late. We take our time with joint compound application, feathering, and sanding to deliver a Level 4 or Level 5 finish depending on your needs.

For residential projects in Bedford and Dartmouth, we typically recommend a Level 4 finish -- smooth, clean, and ready for standard paint. For areas with critical lighting or a high-gloss finish, we'll bring it to Level 5 with a skim coat across the entire surface.

TEXTURE MATCHING

Older homes across Halifax and Sackville often have textured ceilings or walls -- knockdown, orange peel, stipple, or skip trowel patterns. When you're doing a repair in these spaces, matching that existing texture is essential. We replicate the original pattern so the repaired area blends seamlessly with the surrounding surface.
