Our Carpet Services

PROFESSIONAL CARPET
INSTALLATION IN HALIFAX

Whether you are looking to add warmth and comfort to a living room, upgrade a bedroom, or give your staircase a clean, finished look, ETRA Pro Services provides professional carpet installation across Halifax and the Halifax Regional Municipality.

We work with homeowners and property managers throughout Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, and surrounding HRM communities to deliver carpet installs that feel right underfoot and look sharp for years to come. From wall-to-wall broadloom in open floor plans to tightly wrapped carpet stairs that hold up to daily traffic, every install is done with care and precision.

"Carpet should feel like home the moment you step on it. We make sure every seam, every edge, and every corner is done right."

Our team handles full carpet replacement projects as well -- removing old, worn carpet and underpad, prepping the subfloor, and laying new carpet that fits perfectly. We are not satisfied until the lines are clean, the corners are tight, and the finish is seamless.
